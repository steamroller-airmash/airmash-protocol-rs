
This table has the time that it takes missiles to 
despawn before being fired. This is without
missile speed upgrades.

All values were reverse engineered by running
a bot from a digitalocean droplet against
US FFA1.

                 | lifetime (ms)
-----------------|------------------------
predator         | 2300
prowler          | 2270
tornado          | 2536
tornado (triple) | 1572
mohawk           | 2223
goliath          | 3545
